Dual Career Calculator

Dual Income Lifestyle Planner

This calculator, a collaboration between FinMango and Modern Husbands, helps dual-income families and couples evaluate their financial trajectory over the next 15 years. Factors include household income, potential childcare costs, healthcare expenses, basic living expenses, taxes, and retirement savings.

We've pre-populated the fields with example numbers. Feel free to adjust these to match your specific situation.
